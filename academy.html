Welcome to the Academy

Raising the youth of Ilford since 2001

Our academy is a place where young players can come to learn and grow. We are a family-friendly club that is dedicated to providing the best possible education and training for our players. What We Offer

Professional Coaching

Learn from UEFA licensed coaches with years of experience in youth development and professional football.

Modern Facilities

Train on professional-grade pitches with access to gym facilities, recovery rooms, and video analysis suites.

School Support

We believe in education first. Our players receive academic support and mentoring alongside their football development.

Pathway to Pro

Clear pathway from academy to first team with opportunities to train with senior players and compete at the highest level.

Character Building

We develop not just footballers, but leaders. Our program emphasizes teamwork, discipline, and respect.
